DOG BREEDS

Image Breed Description
Shiba Inu A hunting breed native to Japan. It belongs to the Spitz breed of dogs, and is the smallest out of all of them.
Siberian Husky Like the Shiba, the Husky is also part of the Spitz breed. It's a working dog and usually medium-sized. It has a double coat of fur to shield itself from the cold.
Pomeranian Like the previous two, the Pomeranian is also a part of the spitz breed. It is small in size, and classified as a "toy dog". Their predecessors were one of the larger Spitz.
German Shepherd Originally a herding dog, this breed is a working dog. Usually medium to large in size, and often used as sniffer dogs for it's amazing senses.
Golden Retriever Affectionate and with a heart of gold, the Retriever is a medium-sized dog and often domesticated. Its affectionate and gentle nature makes it easy to get along with.
Chihuahua A Mexican breed of toy dog, the smallest of all dog breeds. Usually kept as a companion or for show.
Pug A breed originally from China, small in size, and resembles that of a tiny bulldog. It's characterised by its tail, wrinkly face, and short muzzle.
Dalmatian A medium-sized breed characterised by its long legs and iconic black spots. Though a hunting dog, it could also serve as a working dog.
Doberman A medium to large breed of domesticated dog. Despite its large feet, it isn't heavy-footed, and has a long muzzle. Intelligent, loyal, and alert, it is an ideal breed kept as a companion who can give you protection.
Welsh Corgi A herding dog that originates from Wales. Two breeds under similar names, the other is a descendant of the Spitz breed.
